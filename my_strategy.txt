# STRATEGY REQUIREMENTS
[INDICATORS]
Primary: 50-period Simple Moving Average (SMA50)
Secondary: 200-period Simple Moving Average (SMA200)
Timeframe: 1-Hour Charts

[EXECUTION RULES]
Buy Signal: Trigger a BUY market order when SMA50 crosses ABOVE SMA200.
Sell Signal: Trigger a SELL market order when SMA50 crosses BELOW SMA200.
Target Assets: ["SPY", "QQQ", "AAPL"]

[RISK MANAGEMENT]
Max Position Size: 2% of total account equity per trade.
Safety Default: Hard stop-loss at 1.5% below entry price.
